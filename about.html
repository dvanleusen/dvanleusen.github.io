Capture the moment

Photography has taught me to stop and smell the roses. It is about noticing those details and living the moment a bit longer. Every photograph I take is an extension of my personality, it allows me to show my perspective of the world.

Photography to me is very personal. I am truly inspired by a couple's love story. Every couple is unique, and good photography is about building trust and relationships. Making a connection with the couple is so important, because they can truly be themselves, and have a good time in front of the camera. I want my couples laughing together, having a great time, showing their affections for each other. And it is my job to capture those moments.

When I am shooting, I am looking for light, color, and composition. But ultimately, I am looking for that moment from the couple. I love incorporating movements in my photography, whether it's them dancing, laughing, or even jumping. It's the couple's energy and love that truly makes a photograph real and genuine. And being able to share photographs with my clients is the ultimate reason why I am a photographer.

When I look at my photos, I see real people and real emotions. My photos are vibrant, creative, fresh, and timeless. When I am out in the field, I am bringing every ounce of love, passion, and energy to create the best imaginery I can for my couples. I love what I do because it allows me to express my creativity, meet amazing people and hear their stories, and I couldn't ask for more!

I'm Daniel van Leusen, and I am a wedding photographer.
